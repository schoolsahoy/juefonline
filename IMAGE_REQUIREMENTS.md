# JUEF Website — Image Requirements for Gemini Generation

## Style Direction
All images should feel **warm, aspirational, and cinematic** — think high-end school brochure meets architectural visualization. Indian children (ages 3-18), modern school environments, natural light, earthy tones with pops of green and gold. No stock photo feel. Every image should look like it belongs to ONE school system.

## IMAGE SPECS

- **Format:** PNG
- **Resolution:** 2400 x 1600 px minimum (3:2 landscape)
- **Hero images:** 3200 x 1800 px preferred (wider cinematic ratio)
- **File size target:** Under 1 MB each (compress after generation)
- **Color palette:** Warm naturals, earthy tones. Primary green (#1A6B5E) and gold (#C49A2A) should feel naturally present in environments — not overlaid.
- **Subjects:** Indian children and adults. Realistic skin tones, school uniforms (simple, not branded), modern Indian school environments.
- **Mood:** Warm, aspirational, cinematic. Natural light preferred. No stock photo stiffness.
