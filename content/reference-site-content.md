### Welcome Section

At the Jain Universal Education Foundation, we are dedicated to revolutionizing education by integrating timeless Jain values with cutting-edge educational practices. Our flagship program, SWAYAM, embodies this vision, providing a holistic and inclusive learning experience that prepares our students for the future while staying rooted in our rich cultural heritage.

### Why We Need a New Education System
The Jain community is aspirational and values excellence in education. Despite being only 1% of the population, Jains contribute significantly to the economy. However, there is a growing need for an education system that not only maintains high academic standards but also integrates Jain values, promoting holistic development and preserving cultural heritage.

### Challenges We Face
Our community faces unique challenges such as traditional business practices being abandoned by the youth, erosion of values, family breakups, and marital compatibility issues. Additionally, competition and reservation policies in broader society pose obstacles. Addressing these challenges requires an education system tailored to our specific cultural and social needs.

### The SWAYAM Model
SWAYAM, our innovative education model, combines Jain philosophy with modern educational practices. It provides free, equitable education to all Jain students, integrates entrepreneurial and financial literacy, and emphasizes self-led learning. By focusing on holistic development and cultural preservation, SWAYAM prepares students to meet future challenges and contribute meaningfully to society.

### Features (5 icon cards)

### Our Mission
Our mission is to create 10,000 Jain leaders by 2030. We aim to provide an education system that not only addresses the academic needs of our students but also prepares them to tackle future challenges, uphold Jain values, and contribute positively to society.
