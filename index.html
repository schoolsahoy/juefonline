The Challenge

Education Was Built for the Industrial Age.
We Live in the AI Age.

AI can write essays, solve math, generate code, and pass exams. If machines can do what schools teach, what should schools actually teach?

🤖

85 Million Jobs Displaced

The World Economic Forum projects 85 million jobs displaced by AI by 2025, with 97 million new roles demanding creativity, ethics, and emotional intelligence.

⚠

92% Can't Apply Learning

Studies show the vast majority of graduates struggle to apply classroom knowledge to real-world situations. Schools build test-takers, not problem-solvers.

💡

Only 1 in 4 Are Employable

Despite millions of graduates each year, employers consistently report fewer than 25% are workplace-ready. The classroom-to-career gap is widening.

“Most of what kids currently learn at school will probably be irrelevant by the time they are 40. In order to keep up with the world of 2050, you will need not merely to invent new ideas and products but above all to reinvent yourself again and again.”
Yuval Noah Harari
Historian & Author, 21 Lessons for the 21st Century

A Different Kind of School

The world doesn't need more test-takers.
It needs humans who can think, feel, and lead.

Three distinct schools, each designed for a different stage of growing up. Age-appropriate environments, pedagogy, and mentorship — because a 4-year-old and a 16-year-old don't learn the same way, and shouldn't be taught the same way.

The Shraman Answer

Build What AI Cannot Replicate

Shraman Education develops the four capabilities that make humans irreplaceable in an AI world.

🧠

Anekantwad

How you THINK. See truth from multiple viewpoints. Critical thinking AI fundamentally lacks.

🗣

Syadwad

How you SPEAK. Hold complexity and uncertainty. Nuanced reasoning AI cannot replicate.

📖

Swadhyaya

How you LEARN. Self-directed, lifelong learning. Continuous adaptation for a changing world.

🧘

Dhyana

How you MAINTAIN. Focus, stillness, and deep attention in an age of infinite distraction.

Complete Development

Six Domains. One Complete Human.

Every Shraman student develops across six integrated domains — not just academics, but the full spectrum of human capability.

🧠

Mind (मन)

HOW to think — the operating system. Built through four pedagogical tools: Anekantwad, Syadwad, Swadhyaya, Dhyana.

📚

Gyan (ज्ञान)

WHAT is learned. Knowledge + creativity across languages, sciences, arts, humanities, and Jain scholarship.

⭐

Character (चरित्र)

Panch Mahavratas as transformation mechanism. By graduation, students ARE ethical — not just think about it.

🤝

Sambandh (संबंध)

Relationships through the full arc of life: self, family, society, earth — including marriage, parenting, and legacy.

💪

Body (शरीर)

Shram (dignified labour) + physical development. Nine foundational life skills placed equal to academics.

✨

Soul (आत्मा)

Purpose and meaning that EMERGES when the other five domains align. Not taught — cultivated through alignment.

What Sets Us Apart

Not Another School. A Different Operating System.

Compared to Montessori, IB, Finnish, Waldorf, and Gurukul models, Shraman is the only framework that combines all of these:

Mind as Operating System

First education system to explicitly frame cognitive development as building an OS. Four Mind Tools embedded in every lesson, every day.

Character as Transformation

Not instruction but automated inner regulation. By graduation, the Panch Mahavratas are how students naturally behave, not rules they follow.

Skills = Academics

Nine foundational life skills (cooking, first aid, financial literacy, handiman) placed equal to math and science. Diagnosed and tracked the same way.

The Precipitation Insight

After 8 years of multi-domain observation, a child's true orientation becomes clear. Only genuine JEE candidates go to science. Others find their real groove.

Full Life Arc Preparation

The Sambandh-Jeevan curriculum explicitly prepares for marriage, parenting, household management, elder care, and legacy. Not just college readiness.

Independence by Class XII

By graduation, a Conqueror can move to any city and set up life. Cook, budget, repair, lead, research, and never give up — however hard the problem.
