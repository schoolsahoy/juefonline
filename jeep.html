The JEEP Journey

From Application to School Launch

A structured pathway that takes you from interest to running your own Shraman school.

1

Application & Selection

Submit your application as a couple. We carefully select 20–30 Jain couples from across India based on commitment, values alignment, and entrepreneurial readiness.

2

Immersive Training

Deep dive into the Shraman Framework, Six Domains, Four Mind Tools, and the operational model. Understand the philosophy, pedagogy, and business of running a Shraman school.

3

Launch a Shraman School — or Join a Sangh Network

Build your own school independently, or step into a partner school chain run by a Sangh or Mahatma. Sanghs get trained, JUEF-certified operators; you gain instant community credibility and ready infrastructure. JEEP prepares you for both paths.

4

Planning & Preparation

Work with JUEF to plan your school: location, infrastructure, teacher recruitment, community outreach, and admissions strategy. Use the complete JUEF school setup playbook.

5

Launch Support

Ongoing mentorship, the Knowledge App for daily curriculum delivery, teacher training support, and the full JUEF network behind you as you launch and grow your school.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

The JEEP program includes immersive training for both husband and wife on the Shraman Framework, operational training for running a school, business planning support, and access to the JUEF cohort community. You'll gain everything needed to understand the model and prepare for launching a school.

No prior education experience is required. JEEP is designed to give you everything you need. What we look for is commitment, values alignment, entrepreneurial mindset, and a genuine desire to impact education in your community. The Shraman Framework and JUEF support system provide the education expertise.

Running a school is a family commitment. Our experience shows that schools thrive when both partners understand the vision, philosophy, and operations. The couple format ensures alignment at home, diverse perspectives in decision-making, and a stronger foundation for a sustainable institution.

The first cohort is focused on Jain couples, as the Shraman Framework draws from Jain philosophical traditions, and the initial community network is within the Jain ecosystem. However, the education framework itself creates CAPABLE humans regardless of background — the philosophy is universal, even if the wisdom tradition is specific.

Launching a school requires careful preparation, infrastructure development, teacher training, and community engagement. JEEP prepares you thoroughly so you understand every aspect of the model, the operational requirements, and what it takes to build a sustainable institution that serves your community for generations.

Selection is based on values alignment with the Shraman philosophy, entrepreneurial readiness, community standing, vision for education, and genuine commitment to building institutions. We're looking for founding couples who will build the movement, not just attend a program.

Shraman schools use JUEF Baseline Examinations through Class VIII. For Class X and XII, we’ve partnered with Pearson (international) and NIOS (India). Your students will have fully recognised qualifications at every stage.
