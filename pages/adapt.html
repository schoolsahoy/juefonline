Adapt Shraman Education

You Already Have a School.
Now Give It a Soul.

Integrate the Shraman Framework into your existing institution — the six domains, the four mind tools, the character-building methodology, and the Knowledge App. Without starting from scratch.

The Reality

Most Schools Teach Subjects. Few Build Humans.

You’ve built a school. You have students, teachers, infrastructure. But you feel the gap — the curriculum covers what children should know, but not what they should become. The Shraman Framework fills that gap. Not by replacing what you do — by deepening it across six dimensions most schools never touch.

The Integration

What Changes. What Stays.

This is not a takeover. It’s an upgrade.

What Stays

Your Brand & Identity

Your school name, your reputation, your community relationships. Nothing changes externally.

Your Board Affiliation

CBSE, ICSE, State Board — your existing board affiliation and exam requirements remain fully intact.

Your Staff

Your existing teachers stay. They get trained in Shraman pedagogy — the four mind tools, observation-based assessment, and domain-integrated teaching.

Your Identity

Your positioning remains unchanged. The integration enhances your educational value without disrupting your established institution.

What Gets Added

Six-Domain Framework

Mind, Gyan, Character, Sambandh, Body, and Soul are woven into your existing timetable — not as extra periods, but as a lens through which all teaching happens.

Four Mind Tools

Anekantwad, Syadwad, Swadhyaya, and Dhyana become embedded pedagogical practices — how students think, speak, learn, and focus.

The Knowledge App

Daily pedagogy delivery, child progress tracking across all domains, and AI-based evaluation. Real-time visibility for teachers, parents, and administrators.

Character Building Methodology

Panch Mahavratas integrated as lived values — front-loaded in early years, becoming inner regulators by senior school.

Teacher Transformation

Comprehensive training to shift from content delivery to mentorship, from testing to observation, from marks to multi-domain development.

Same School. Deeper Education. Your parents will notice the difference. Your teachers will feel it. And your students will become it — more thoughtful, more grounded, more capable than any exam score could measure.

How It Works

A Phased Integration

Not a big bang. A thoughtful, phased transformation.

1

Assessment

JUEF studies your school — current curriculum, teaching methods, infrastructure, and community expectations. We identify where the Shraman Framework maps naturally and where shifts are needed.

2

Teacher Training

Your teachers are trained in Shraman pedagogy — the four mind tools, domain-integrated teaching, observation-based assessment, and the Knowledge App. This is the foundation of everything.

3

Curriculum Integration

The six domains are layered into your existing timetable. Character, Mind, Sambandh, and Body components are woven alongside your board curriculum — not in addition to, but through it.

4

Knowledge App Deployment

The app goes live — delivering daily pedagogy, tracking child progress across all six domains, and giving parents real visibility into their child’s holistic development.

5

Ongoing Partnership

Curriculum updates, teacher refresher programs, app improvements, and quality reviews. You’re joining a living educational ecosystem committed to continuous improvement.

You built the school. Let us help you build the education. The infrastructure is the easy part. The harder question is: what kind of human are you developing? The Shraman Framework answers that question with 2,500 years of clarity.
