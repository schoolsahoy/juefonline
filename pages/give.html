02

Jain RTE

Help a Child Learn at a Shraman School.

Not every family can afford a Shraman education. The Jain Right to Education initiative ensures that deserving children from economically weaker backgrounds get access to the same six-domain, character-building, purpose-driven education as everyone else.

Your sponsorship places a child in a Shraman school — with the full curriculum, the same mentors, and the same expectations. No dilution. No compromise. Just equal opportunity to become a capable human.

The Jain RTE Program launches in January 2027.

Be the first to know when registrations open.

The Ripple Effect

One Child. One Family. One Community.

When you contribute to the Shraman Education mission, you don’t just change one life. You change how a family thinks about learning, how a community sees its children’s potential, and how the next generation carries itself through life.

Every rupee builds curriculum, trains educators, and opens doors for children who deserve a complete education.
