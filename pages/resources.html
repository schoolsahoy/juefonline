For School Owners & Trustees

Affiliate with Shraman Education

What We Provide

When you affiliate with the Shraman Education network, you receive comprehensive support across every aspect of running a school:

Management Training — Administration, leadership, financial management, and strategic planning.

Teacher Training — Professional development, pedagogical strategies, and integrating Jain values into teaching.

Parent Orientation — Helping parents understand and support their children's learning journey.

Curriculum — A robust curriculum that blends Jain values with modern educational standards.

SWAYAM School Status — Recognition as part of a unique educational model with ongoing support.

Affiliation Levels

Schools can affiliate at any level and grow with the Shraman network:

Early Childhood (Nursery–UKG)

Balanced approach to play and learning with Jain values for ethical development from an early age.

Primary (Class I–V)

Foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking with cultural education and character building.

Middle Years (Class VI–VIII)

Deeper exploration with independent learning, problem-solving, and leadership skills.

High School (Class IX–XII)

Advanced coursework, career guidance, and specialized wings preparing students for the modern world.
