The Schools

Start With One. Scale to Three.

Most chains begin with Kalash and expand as the community grows.

Kalash School

Nursery – Class II  |  Ages 3–8

The foundation. Sensory activation, character seeding, play-based learning, zero exams. The entry point for most school chains.

Requires infrastructure and planning

Padam School

Class III – VIII  |  Ages 8–14

The growth years. Five-Stage Learning, Go Wide exploration, self-directed learning up to 30%. One Padam for every 5 Kalash schools.

Larger campus required

Shikhar School

Class IX – XII  |  Ages 14–18

The summit. Six specialised wings, residential model, PhD-level mentors, competitive exam preparation. The capstone of the chain.

Residential campus required

The Process

How to Start a School Chain with JUEF

1

Initial Conversation

Reach out to JUEF. We discuss your vision, community size, geographic focus, and alignment with the Shraman philosophy. This is a partnership — both sides must be the right fit.

2

Partnership Agreement

Formalise the design and curriculum partnership. Define the scope: how many schools, which levels (Kalash/Padam/Shikhar), your brand identity, and the operational framework.

3

Design & Build

JUEF provides architectural guidelines, classroom design blueprints, and environment specifications. You build the campus. We ensure every space is optimised for Shraman learning.

4

Train & Equip

Your teachers go through JUEF’s comprehensive training program. The Knowledge App is deployed. Curriculum materials, assessment frameworks, and operational playbooks are handed over.

5

Launch & Grow

Your first school opens under your brand with the full Shraman system running. JUEF provides ongoing curriculum updates, app improvements, and quality support as you scale to more schools.
