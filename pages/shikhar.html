Shikhar School — Summit

Go Deep. Go Hard.

Class IX to XII. Ages 14–18. Two stages — Achievers and Conquerors. Six specialized wings. PhD-level mentors. A residential program that produces humans ready to conquer anything.

The Shikhar Philosophy

Win the War, Not Just Battles

After going wide at Padam, Shikhar students go deep and then go hard. The Precipitation Insight has revealed each student's true orientation. Now they specialize with intensity, guided by professional mentors who are experts in their fields — not just teachers.

Self-directed learning reaches 50–70%. Students create research, solve real-world problems, and build the three D's: Dedication, Discipline, and Diligence. By graduation, a Conqueror can move to any city and set up life — cook, budget, repair, lead, research, and never give up.

“Boil things down to their fundamental truths and reason up from there, as opposed to reasoning by analogy. That’s the essence of critical thinking.”
Elon Musk
CEO, Tesla & SpaceX

The Progression

Achiever to Conqueror

🎯

Achievers — Go DEEP

Class IX–X • Age 14–16

Build on Character and Mind domain proficiency from Kalash and Padam. Add SHRAM — build GRIT through extended Swadhyaya hours. 30–50% self-led learning. Transition from "go wide" to "dig deep for your answers." Read more, listen more. Solve REAL-WORLD problems.

👑

Conquerors — Go HARD

Class XI–XII • Age 16–18

Build the 3 D's: Dedication, Discipline, Diligence. 50–70% self-led learning. Win the WAR, not battles. Work deep and hard. Reference EXPERTS through personal mentorship (1:10–12 ratio), research papers, and industry connections. Create research. Never give up.

Specialized Academies

Six Wings of Excellence

Each wing is a specialized academy with dedicated labs, professional mentors, and a clear competitive focus.

🤖

AI, Robotics & Technology

Robotics workbenches, AI/ML coding arena, IoT and drone studio. Mentors: IIT/IIIT engineers, AI researchers, startup founders.

JEE Advanced, Hackathons, Coding Olympiads

🧬

Pharmaceutical & Life Sciences

Biology, chemistry, and biotech labs with PCR and electrophoresis equipment. Mentors: AIIMS/NIPER researchers, pharma scientists.

NEET/AIIMS Prep, Bio-Olympiads

💰

Business, Finance & Entrepreneurship

Finance trading simulation, startup incubator, case-study studio. Mentors: IIM/ISB alumni, CAs, entrepreneurs, VCs.

CAT Prep, Business Plan Competitions

🎨

Art, Design & Music

Fine arts studio, music conservatory, performing arts stage. Mentors: NID/NIFT graduates, professional artists, musicians.

Portfolios, Exhibitions, Recitals

⚖

Legal, Liberal Arts & Admin

Moot court, model parliament, humanities research room. Mentors: NLU lawyers, IAS officers, policy researchers.

CLAT, UPSC Prep, MUNs, Debates

🏅

Sports & Defence

Sports science lab, indoor arena, parade ground, shooting range. Mentors: Sports scientists, ex-servicemen, national athletes.

NDA/CDS Prep, National Athletics

Not Teachers. Mentors.

1:10 ratio. PhD-level expertise. Real-world practitioners.

Shikhar mentors are not classroom teachers. They are IIT engineers, AIIMS researchers, IIM alumni, NLU lawyers, national athletes, and working professionals who guide students through personal mentorship.

Residential Program

The Shikhar Daily Rhythm

A residential experience designed for deep work, discipline, and complete immersion.

🌅

Morning

Fitness and meditation. Strengthening body and mind before the day begins. Dhyana practice and physical conditioning.

☀

Day

Academic labs, research, group seminars, and collaborative inquiry within specialized wings. Deep work with expert mentors.

🌙

Evening

Self-study, reflection, mentorship circles, and competitive exam preparation. The quiet hours where grit is built.

Assessment Philosophy

How Grit and the War Mindset Are Assessed

Not academic metrics. Not test scores. Real measures of human capability.

Hard Work

How much effort is being put in, measured through observable engagement and persistence.

End Results

Tangible outputs — research papers, projects, presentations, competitions won.

Goal Achievability

Setting ambitious goals and the ability to reach them through sustained effort.

Work → Rework

Iteration count and persistence. How many times you rework to achieve excellence.
